A good Burger is the best thing ever. No more words needed.

I do not have any idea why anyone could ever disagree that its just the best.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Meat or veggie patties
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Go and grap all the ingredients at the supermarket
  2. Put the meat in a pan and fry it.
  3. Burger buns go in the oven for a few minutes
  4. Cut the rest in good pieces
  5. Now put everything together and enjoy

Enjoy your Burger!
